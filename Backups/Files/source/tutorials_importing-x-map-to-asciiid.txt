++ Step 1: Download the X map

Starting from [[[wiki:versions:X5|X5]]] until [[[wiki:versions:X12|X12]]], maps are stored at  asciicker.com/<version>/images/map.png, and [[[wiki:versions:X13]]] image is stored as [http://asciicker.com/x13/images/map2.png map2.png]. Just download this image, and you have the map.

++ Step 2: Put it in the maps folder
In your local version, ([[[tutorials:download local version]]]), put the map picture into the "maps" directory (you may have to create this). Rename this to "new.png".

++ Step 3: Import it into asciiid
Open up asciiid, and under the "View" menu, press "New'.
