+ Description
This one is more different than X2. It has shaded flat lands. This also features a map in the /images directory, something that is going to update later.

++ Features
Moving via WASD and arrow keys
Click and drag movement
Friction when you stop moving is slippery
Pressing Q and E rotates your perspective up to 12 angles
Can climb up any hill at ease
Flat-ish hills
Map rendering version 1
