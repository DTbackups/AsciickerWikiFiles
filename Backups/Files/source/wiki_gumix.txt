**Gumix** (/guːmɪks/; Polish: /ɡumiks/) is a Polish software developer, he is the creator and developer of [*https://ascii-patrol.com/ Ascii Patrol] and [*https://asciicker.com Asciicker]. He is developing them as hobby projects. He is employed as the lead developer for a graphics engine designed for gambling machines^^[((bibcite job))]^^. He was born on the 29th of December^^[((bibcite bday))][((bibcite bdaybetter))]^^, but he has not stated the year.
